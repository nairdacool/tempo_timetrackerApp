Updated March 2026
Role: Admin
Web App (Desktop + Mobile)
What is Tempo?
Your team's time-tracking and approval hub

Tempo is a time-tracking application designed for teams. Members log their daily work hours, and Admins review, approve, and report on that time across projects and clients.

Regular Member

Logs time, views their own timesheet, browses projects, and sees personal reports.

Admin (You)

Everything a member can do, plus approving timesheets, managing the team, projects, organizations, and viewing team-wide reports.

Admin-only sections in this manual are marked with Admin Only. These pages only appear in your navigation sidebar.
Logging In
How to access your account

First-Time Login (New Invite)

  1. Check your email You will receive an invitation email with a Set Password link.
  2. Click the link and set your password Your password must be at least 8 characters long.
  3. You're in! You will be redirected to the Dashboard automatically.

Regular Login

  1. Enter your email and password Use the email address you were invited with.
  2. Click Sign In You will land on the Dashboard.

Signing Out

Click your profile area at the bottom-left of the sidebar and select Sign out.

If your account is deactivated by another admin, you will be automatically signed out and shown a message. Contact your organization owner to restore access.

Dashboard
Team-wide overview at a glance

The Dashboard is your home screen. As an Admin it shows team-wide numbers instead of just your own.

Stat Cards (top row)

CardWhat it shows
Team This WeekTotal hours logged by the entire team this week, with an up/down vs last week indicator.
Team This MonthTotal hours logged by all team members this calendar month.
Active ProjectsNumber of projects currently in active status.
Pending ApprovalsNumber of timesheets awaiting your review. A red arrow means action is needed.

Timer Widget

Use the Timer to track time in real time. Hit Start to begin, then Stop to save the entry. You will be prompted to choose a project and add a description.

Recent Entries

A scrollable list of the most recent time entries across all team members. Each entry shows the member's avatar, project, description, date, duration, and approval status.

Click the pencil icon on any entry to edit or delete it.

Hours Chart

A bar chart showing logged hours per day or per week. Helps you spot unusually busy or quiet periods at a glance.

Project Breakdown

A summary table listing each project, total hours logged, budget hours, and how much of the budget has been consumed (shown as a progress bar).

Click Pending Approvals on the stat card or use the sidebar to jump straight to the Approvals page when you need to review timesheets.
Timesheet
Log, edit, and submit weekly hours

The Timesheet page is organized week by week. Use the arrow buttons at the top to navigate between weeks. Each week runs from Monday to Sunday.

Adding a Time Entry

  1. Select the date Click on the day column where you want to add time, or use the form at the top.
  2. Fill in the entry form Choose a Project, write a Description, and set the Start time and End time. The duration is calculated automatically.
  3. Click Save The entry appears under the correct day in the timesheet list.

Editing or Deleting an Entry

Click on any entry to open the Edit Entry modal. From there you can:

  • Change the project, description, or times.
  • Delete the entry by clicking Delete Entry (shown in red).
Approved entries cannot be edited or deleted. If an approved entry needs to change, you must first reject the entire week submission from the Approvals page, then make the correction.

Submitting a Week for Approval

  1. Navigate to the week you want to submit Use the week navigator arrows.
  2. Click "Submit Week for Approval" The button appears at the top of the timesheet view. All Draft entries in that week change to Pending.

Entry Status Meanings

StatusMeaning
◌ DraftSaved but not yet submitted. Still editable.
⏳ PendingSubmitted, waiting for Admin approval.
✓ ApprovedReviewed and approved by an Admin. Locked.
✗ RejectedSent back by Admin. Edit and resubmit.

Exporting Your Timesheet

Click the Export CSV button at the top-right of the Timesheet to download all visible entries as a spreadsheet.

Admin: Viewing Another Member's Timesheet

As an Admin, use the member selector dropdown at the top of the Timesheet page to switch between team members and inspect or edit their entries directly.

Projects
Create and manage client projects

Projects are the buckets that time entries belong to. Every time entry must be assigned to a project. Admins can create, edit, and archive projects.

Creating a New Project

  1. Click "+ New Project" The button is in the top-right of the Projects page.
  2. Fill in the project details
    • Name – A clear, descriptive project name.
    • Client – Select an existing client or type a new one.
    • Color – A color dot that appears on time entries for quick recognition.
    • Budget (hours) – Optional maximum hours for this project.
    • Billable – Toggle on if hours should count toward billing.
    • Status – Start as Active.
  3. Click Create The project appears in the grid immediately.

Editing a Project

Click the ⋯ menu on any project card (or click the card itself) to open the Edit Project modal. You can change the name, color, budget, billable flag, client, or status.

Project Statuses

StatusMeaning
ActiveCurrently in progress. Members can log time against it.
On HoldTemporarily paused.
CompletedWork is done. Hidden from the default view.
ArchivedNo longer relevant. Hidden from the default view.
Completed and Archived projects are hidden by default. Click "Show completed & archived" to reveal them.

Filtering & Searching

Use the toolbar at the top of the page to:

  • Search by project name.
  • Filter by Client – select a specific client from the dropdown.
  • Filter by Status – narrow by Active, On Hold, etc.
  • Switch View – toggle between Grid view and "By Client" grouped view.

Budget Tracking

Each project card displays a progress bar showing how many hours have been logged versus the budget. When the budget is close to being exceeded the bar turns red.

Deleting a project is permanent. It will remove the project record. Time entries previously logged against it will retain a reference but the project will no longer appear in lists. Prefer Archiving instead.
Reports

The Reports page gives you a full picture of where your team's time is going. As an Admin, you see data for all team members — not just yourself.

Switch between three views using the tabs below the chart:

Use the export buttons in the top-right area:

Approvals Admin Only
Review and approve weekly timesheet submissions

When a team member clicks "Submit Week for Approval", their timesheet appears here. Your job is to review the hours and either approve or reject them.

Understanding the Approval Queue

Each card in the list represents one member's submitted week. It shows:

  • The member's name and avatar.
  • The week range (e.g. "Mar 3 – Mar 9, 2026").
  • Total hours logged that week.
  • A mini breakdown of hours per project.
  • Current status: Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

Filtering the List

Use the tabs at the top of the page:

TabShows
Pending (n)Timesheets waiting for your action. Start here.
Approved (n)Already approved submissions.
Rejected (n)Submissions you sent back.
All (n)Everything, regardless of status.

Approving a Single Timesheet

  1. Click the card or "View Details" A modal opens showing every time entry for that week.
  2. Review the entries Check projects, descriptions, and hours for accuracy.
  3. Click "Approve" All entries in that week are marked Approved and locked.

Rejecting a Timesheet

  1. Open the approval card Click "View Details" to see the full breakdown.
  2. Click "Reject" A text field appears asking for a rejection reason.
  3. Type the reason and confirm The member will see their submission returned as Rejected with your note. They can then correct and resubmit.

Bulk Approve — All Pending at Once

When you are on the Pending tab and there are timesheets waiting, a banner appears at the top with an "Approve All" button. Clicking it approves every pending submission in one action — useful at the end of a payroll period.

Approving is reversible only by rejecting. If you approve a timesheet and later discover an error, open the approval card and click Reject to send it back for correction.
Team Admin Only
Invite, manage, and deactivate team members

Inviting a New Member

  1. Click "+ Invite Member" The button is in the top-right of the Team page.
  2. Enter their email address This is the address the invite will be sent to.
  3. Assign a role Choose from: Admin, Developer, Designer, or Engineer. Only assign Admin to people who should manage the team.
  4. Click "Send Invite" The new member receives an email with a link to set their password and join the team. Their card appears in the grid with a Pending Invite status until they accept.

Member Card Info

Each member card shows:

  • Avatar (initials + color), full name, role, and email.
  • This Week / This Month hours.
  • Last seen – when they last had the app open.
  • Status: Active, Pending Invite, or Inactive.

Editing a Member

Click the Edit button on any member card to open the Edit Member modal. You can:

  • Change their name.
  • Change their role.
  • Change their avatar color.
  • Deactivate the account (they will be signed out and cannot log back in).
  • Reactivate a previously deactivated account.

Filtering & Sorting

Use the toolbar at the top of the Team page:

  • Search by member name.
  • Filter by Role – All Roles, Admin, Developer, Designer, Engineer.
  • Sort – by Name, by Week Hours (highest first), or by Month Hours (highest first).
  • Show/Hide Inactive – inactive members are hidden by default but can be revealed.
Deactivating a member signs them out immediately. Any unsaved time entries on their device will be lost. Deactivation does not delete their historical data.
Organizations Admin Only
Group members and projects into organizational units

Organizations let you group members and projects into separate units — for example, different departments, subsidiaries, or client teams.

Creating an Organization

  1. Click "+ New Organization" Enter a name for the organization.
  2. Click Create The organization card appears with empty Members and Projects sections.

Managing an Organization Card

Each organization card shows:

  • The organization name and creation date.
  • A list of members belonging to it (with their roles).
  • A list of projects assigned to it (with status).

Click the Edit (pencil) icon on a card to:

  • Rename the organization.
  • Delete the organization (members and projects are not deleted, just unlinked).

Removing a Member or Project from an Org

Inside a card, click the × button next to a member or project to unlink them from that organization. This does not delete the member or project.

Organizations are organizational labels only. Removing an entity from an org does not affect their time entries, approvals, or access to the app.
Settings
Manage your personal profile and password

Settings is your personal account page. Changes here only affect your own account. To manage another member's details, use the Team page instead.

Profile Section

  • Full Name – The name displayed across the app.
  • Initials – Auto-derived from your name, but can be customised (up to 3 characters). This is what appears in your avatar circle.
  • Avatar Color – Pick from a palette of preset colors for your avatar.

Click Save Changes to apply. The sidebar and your avatar update immediately.

Password Section

  1. Enter your new password Must be at least 8 characters.
  2. Confirm the password Both fields must match exactly.
  3. Click "Update Password" You will see a confirmation message.
Use the eye icon () next to the password field to toggle password visibility while typing.
Tips & Common Workflows
Work faster and avoid common mistakes

End-of-Week Admin Checklist

  1. Check the Approvals badge The number on the sidebar tells you how many timesheets are waiting.
  2. Go to Approvals → Pending tab Review each submission. Use "View Details" for any that look unusual.
  3. Approve or Reject Approve correct timesheets. Reject any with errors and provide a clear reason.
  4. Run a Report Go to Reports → This Month or Custom to confirm totals before billing or payroll.
  5. Export if needed Use Export PDF for client-ready reports or Export Detail CSV for accounting systems.

Quick-Reference Tips

SituationWhat to do
A member can't log in Go to Team → find their card → check if they are set to Inactive → click Edit → Reactivate.
A project is over budget Go to Projects → click the project → increase the Budget (hours) or change status to On Hold.
A member logged time on the wrong project Go to Timesheet → select their name → find the entry → click Edit → change the project.
Need hours for a specific date range Go to Reports → select Custom → set From/To → click Apply.
Want to see who worked the most this month Go to Reports → By Member tab → entries are sorted by hours.
Need to onboard a new employee Go to Team → Invite Member → enter email and role → Send Invite.
A member left the company Go to Team → Edit their card → Deactivate the account.
Need to send a billing report to a client Go to Reports → filter to the relevant period → Export PDF.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Using "Approve All" without reviewing first — always scan the Pending list before bulk-approving, especially if any card shows an unusually high or low number of hours.
Deleting a project with historical entries — use Archive instead to preserve the data.
Deactivating a member mid-week — they will be signed out immediately. Notify them first so they can save and submit any open timesheets.
